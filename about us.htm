FAQ

Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage or any other page, enter your details and a secure password. Free basics access is available immediately; learning features require a subscription.

We offer a free tier with limited access to core lessons. Premium plans (monthly or annual) unlock unlimited videos, downloadable worksheets, live tutoring sessions, and ad-free browsing. Visit our pricing page for details.

Premium subscribers can download videos, worksheets, and study guides for offline use via the mobile app. Free users don't have the download options. An active internet connection is required for live sessions, and real-time progress syncing.
